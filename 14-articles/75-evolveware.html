Oak Technical Services is pleased to have been chosen as a licensed reseller of the EvolveWare Legacy Modernizer Platform™. This software platform can dramatically reduce the time and effort required to migrate off a legacy system by extracting business rules and assisting in the migration. 

Why Legacy Modernizer?

Successful organizations are those that are able to make informed decisions across their business so as to control costs, deliver excellent customer service and respond to market and technology challenges. As Information Technology systems become increasingly complex, customer expectations grow, and the demands of regulators become ever more onerous, the ability to understand, manage and modernize applications within a flexible infrastructure is more important than ever.

Imagine a platform that provides automation to every aspect of a software portfolio life-cycle: from maintenance, to optimization, to modernization. 

Imagine that this platform:

  • Can be installed on-site, or at your data center or at your service provider’s facilities
  • Is web-based and hence accessible to authorized personnel via the intranet or internet
  • Generates automated documentation of software applications that are currently written in a wide range of different languages
  • Is customizable to support additional languages
  • Enables on-going automated update of documentation of your entire software application portfolio
  • Presents documentation in various formats to facilitate the training of support personnel, the creation of multi-disciplinary teams and the delivery of artifacts that are understandable and valuable to business analysts
  • Performs impact analysis on applications in response to change requests and bug fixes, thereby allowing the user to determine the true consequences of changes and helping to create more realistic time and cost estimates
  • Gives support personnel the capabilities to keep your portfolio optimized and thus more relevant to your business
  • Provides features to extract business rules and processes so that a path to modernization for each application may be charted
  • Allows re-factoring of extracted logic in preparation for transformation to more modern, supportable and efficient environments
  • Automatically exports application business rules to state-of-the-art third party products such as Business Rule Engines (BRE), Business Process Management (BPM) tools and Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) platforms
  • Provides a high-degree of automated transformation of applications written in legacy languages to applications written in modern languages that are maintainable, scalable and have no run-time dependencies

Imagine a platform that can perform Application Maintenance, Application Rationalization, Application Consolidation and Application Modernization.

Imagine EvolveWare’s Legacy Modernizer™ Platform.
